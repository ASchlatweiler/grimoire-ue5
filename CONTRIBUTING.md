# Contributing to Grimoire (UE MCP Server)

Thanks for your interest. A few things to set expectations:

---

## This is a solo project

Grimoire is developed and maintained by one person, around other work and life. There is no roadmap commitment, no SLA on responses, and no guarantee that PRs will be reviewed or merged — even good ones.

That's not a slight. It's just honest.

---

## What's welcome

- **Bug reports** — If something is broken, open an issue with reproduction steps and your UE version. I'll look at it when I can.
- **Feature requests** — Open an issue. No promises, but I do read them.
- **Forks** — Go build something. That's what the license is for.

## What to expect from PRs

Pull requests are accepted at my discretion and on my timeline. If you submit one:

- Keep it focused. One thing per PR.
- Don't refactor things I didn't ask you to refactor.
- If it's a big change, open an issue first so we're not both wasting time.

I may close PRs without comment if they don't fit the direction of the project. It's not personal.
